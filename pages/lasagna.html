World's Best Lasagna!

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat: 1 pound sweet Italian sausage and ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic:
    1. ½ cup minced onion
    2. 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Tomato Products:
    1. 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    2. 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
    3. 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • Water: ½ cup water
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings:
    1. 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
    2. 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
    3. 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
    4. 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    5. ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
    6. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: 12 lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses:
    1. 16 ounces ricotta cheese
    2. ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
    3. ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Egg: 1 egg

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
