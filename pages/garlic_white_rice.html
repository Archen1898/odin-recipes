Garlic White Rice

Description

Flavorful white rice seasoned to perfection with garlic, olive oil, and Salt

Ingredients

  • White Rice: Any brand of white rice (preferably Jasmine rice for added flavor)
  • Garlic: Two or three garlic cloves
  • Olive Oil: Preferred brand of olive oil
  • Salt: Preferred brand of salt

Directions

  1. Mince fresh garlic cloves and heat up oil in pot
  2. Prepare 1 cup of white rice to add to pot
  3. Add minced garlic cloves to pot and season with salt to taste
  4. Allow cloves to fry for about 15 to 30 seconds depending on temperature of the pot. Once it begins to slightly brown, remove pot from heat and add white rice
  5. Mix Rice into garlic and oil until completely mixed. Once done add two cups of water to the pot.
  6. Place pot back on stove and bring to boil
  7. Once pot it brought to a boil, reduce heat until rice is tender
  8. Enjoy!
